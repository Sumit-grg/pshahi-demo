Introduction

Meet Pradeep, a seasoned writer-director boasting over 6 years of experience in the film industry. His journey began with directing short films, steadily earning acclaim for his knack in translating inspiring tales of the Gurkhas onto the silver screen. Recently, his debut film "1915 The Legend of the Gurkhas" premiered on Amazon Prime, highlighting his unique vision and storytelling prowess. Additionally, he has just completed the theatrical release of his second feature, "Antim Sanskar: The Last Ritual."

Hailing from Nepal and now established as a British filmmaker, Pradeep infuses his works with a unique perspective born from his multicultural background. Currently immersed in his third feature project, "Eklo - 1" following the success of his second feature film, "Antim Sanskar," Pradeep exhibits an unwavering passion for filmmaking and a dedication to crafting stories that deeply resonate with audiences. He is recognized as a rising talent in the Nepalese film industry. As a director, Pradeep's primary goal is to craft films that forge deep connections with viewers on emotional and intellectual levels. He firmly believes in cinema's extraordinary ability to transport audiences to diverse realms, perspectives, and experiences, making it an incredibly potent medium for storytelling. His passion lies in exploring intricate themes and characters that both challenge and inspire audiences. Having lived in both Nepal and the UK, Pradeep has personally witnessed the transformative power of cinema. It is his mission to leverage his expertise to benefit Nepal and its future generations by undertaking increasingly ambitious and expansive projects. His ultimate aim is to present Nepalese stories to a global audience, offering fresh perspectives on the country's rich culture and its people.
